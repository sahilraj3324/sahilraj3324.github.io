ABOUT US

Happy Shopping! Easy Shopping

We offers the users to search and shop their desired item here from different applications in a single go with extra benefits of getting various amazing offers and suggesting you the best among all the sear results. Spider Monkey ,SM, is a web scrapper which helps the users to find their desired product in less time and in one go. It enhances the activity of online shopping by presenting results from various sites and comparing the products by keeping ourselves on the place of the buyers which helps them to choose better quality product in less time and without less confusion. We try to work as a left hand of our customers and go on searching for them and end up suggesting them the best product in a few seconds. Doesn't this name makes you curious of knowing the logic behind it? Spider Monkey is a very logical name which indicates that our search engine is just like a monkey which jumps from different sites to applications and collect data for our users’ searches and design a web like a spider of processing the data and suggesting the user for buying the product. Being motivated by these two species we’ve designed our product in a similar manner. We would like to embrace our website by telling our users the motive behind this site which is just a project, a mandatory project, for which we weren’t serious at all but it ended up being a valuable product for which we’ll work even harder and would bring some more affirmative changes for our users. We as learners know the value of taking risks, believing on someone, and so we kept our users in our minds to gain your trust and just wanted our customers to enjoy shopping using our scrapper, Spider Monkey.
